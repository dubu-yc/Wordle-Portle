How to play

Welcome to Wordle- the original word-guessing game!
The goal of the game is to guess the WORDLE in six tries.
Each guess must be a valid five-letter word.
Hit the enter button to submit each guess.
After each guess, you will receive hints to show how close your guess was to the correct answer.
Green means that your letter was in the correct space.
Yellow means that your letter is in the word but not in the correct space.
Black means that the letter is not in the word at all.
