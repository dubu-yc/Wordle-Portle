How to play

Welcome to Nerdle- an arithmetic-based spinoff of the wildy successful Wordle!
The goal of the game is to guess the NERDLE in six tries.
Each guess must be a valid eight character equation
Hit the enter button to submit each guess.
After each guess, you will receive hints to show how close your guess was to the correct answer.
Green means that your number or operation was in the correct space.
Yellow means that your number or operation is in the word but not in the correct space.
Black means that the number or operation is not in the word at all.
