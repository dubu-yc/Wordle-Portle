Yordle




Welcome to Yordle- a spinoff of the wildly successful Wordle!
The goal of the game is to guess the League of Legends champion in six tries.
Each guess must be a valid League of Legends champion name, with punctuation with proper capitalization. (I.E. Cho'Gath is Cho'Gath not Chogath or Cho'gath or Cho)
Hit the enter button to submit each guess.
After each guess, you will receive hints to show how close your guess was to the correct answer.
